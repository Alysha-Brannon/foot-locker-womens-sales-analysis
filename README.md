# foot-locker-womens-sales-analysis
## Foot Locker Women's Sales Analysis 2021

### Project Goal
Analyze women's product sales at Foot Locker in 2021 across 
products, months, states, and sales channels.

### Tools Used
Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Calculated Columns)

### Dataset
Adidas Sales Dataset — Kaggle (1,147 rows)

### Key Findings
1. Women's Apparel was the top selling product category
2. Sales and Units Sold both peaked in December 2021
3. South Carolina led all states in Total Sales and Units Sold
4. Online was the top channel in Sales, Units, and Profit Margin
